How It Works

Get Your Passport in 5 Simple Steps

Our streamlined process ensures secure, transparent passport processing

01
📝

Submit Application

Fill out your details, upload documents, book an appointment slot, and submit your application online.

02
📋

Document Verification

Admin verifies each uploaded document individually. Failed docs can be re-uploaded by the applicant.

03
🛡️

Police Verification

Local police verifies the applicant's background using Aadhaar-based criminal database checks.

04
✅

Admin Approval

After police clearance, admin gives final approval. This is the last gate before passport issuance.

05
🛂

Receive Passport

Your passport is printed and issued. Track status in real-time and download your application as PDF.
